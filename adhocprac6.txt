practical 6: creating an adhoc network

take 2 wireless router(wrt300n)
connect both using (copper cross cable)

go wireless router0 and in wireless give 
			network name ssid:cs
			security:wpa2 personal
			name passsphrase:ciscorouter0 and save
			go to administration and enable remote management

go wireless router1 and in wireless give 
			network name ssid:IT
			security:wpa2 personal
			name passsphrase:ciscorouter1 and save
			go to administration and enable remote management

Now take 5 pcs and go to all pcs and switch off and replace

now go to pc0 and in desktop->pc wireless->connect->refresh->click cs and give password:ciscorouter0 and connect
same for all pcs
